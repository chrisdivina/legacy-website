Portfolio

You will find here some examples of my most recent work. I have also worked with several brands including L'Oreal, Lindt, Kipling, and Thompson.

  • Trendstop

    I am currently a Senior Web Developer for Trendstop, where I mainly focus on front-end development. While my role is to work with designers to implement new designs and functionalities, I have recently been more involved in optimising some web pages to improve performance. Being a player in the fashion industry, Trendstop relies on heavy use of high definition images, which is a challenge in terms of performance. I have worked on regular dynamic pages, web applications, as well as functionalities for administrators.

    Technology: JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, PHP, CSS, SASS, Gulp, Webpack

    • Creation of web applications, such as Trendstop Stories

    • Asynchronous and gradual loading of high-definition images

    • Mobile-friendly responsive layout

  • The Culture Trip

    I joined The Culture Trip in its early days, when it was a very small startup. They needed someone to look after their website to please their rapidily increasing number of visitors, and to smooth the editorial process. As the Head of UX & Front-End Development, I was in charge to revamp the whole website, migrate 10,000+ articles from an XML-based CMS to a Wordpress database, as well as create several tools to improve the editorial production workflow.

    Technology: PHP, JavaScript, jQuery, AJAX, AngularJS, CSS
